Rapper History

Rapper sword dancing is a tradition from North East England. There are teams dancing rapper across the UK, and even Europe and America. The dance is performed by teams of 5 people accompanied by a musician and usually a couple of characters, the Tommy and Betty. It is usually performed around pubs in the local area, though every so often there are national and international competitions.

What do you need to start a rapper team?

  • 5 Dancers
  • At least 1 musician
  • A set of 5 swords plus spares
  • Hard soled shoes

For more information, check out Rapper Dancing on Wikipedia, or at Rapper.org.uk.
